Welcome to La Pizza - Roseland's number one choice for pizza and Italian food! We pride ourselves on providing the highest levels of customer service, and we only use the absolute freshest, best quality ingredients for all of our dishes. From pasta to pizza to antipasti, La Pizza has something for each and every palette. The Cataldi family recipes have been handed down from generation to generation, and we hope you and your family will continue to enjoy our rich tradtion of Italian cooking for years to come.

    We know there are many choices out there -- from everyone at La Pizza in Roseland, we graciously extend our sincere appreciation for your support and patronage. Our goal is to always provide you with a truly satisfying eating experience.

    Do you have an upcoming event or party? Leave the details for all of your special occasions to the expert caterers at La Pizza. Whether you are planning a corporate luncheon, family gathering, or just an old-fashioned pizza night, La Pizza will ensure that your day will be truly distinctive and memorable.
